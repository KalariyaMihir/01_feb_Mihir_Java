-> Use case diagram is graphical depiction of a use's possible interactions with a system.

-> A use case diagram shows various use cases and different types of users the system has and will often be accompanied by other types of diagrams as well.

-> The use cases are represented by either circles or ellipses.

-> The actors are often shown as stick figures.

-> A Use Case diagram can help provide a higher level view of the system.

-> " USE-CASE diagrams are the blueprints for your system.

-> Due to simplistic nature, use case diagrams can be a good communication tool for stakeholders.
