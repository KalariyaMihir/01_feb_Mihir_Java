* what is Deference Between OOP & POP ?

-> OOP :

- OOP Stands for Object Oriented Programming
- OOP Bottom Up approach
- OOP is More secure than POP
- Language that follows OOPs Concept C++,Java,Python..

                ----------

-> POP :

- POP stands for Procedure Oriented Programming
- POP Follows Top to Bottom approach
- POP is not secure .
- Language that follows POP concept C.
